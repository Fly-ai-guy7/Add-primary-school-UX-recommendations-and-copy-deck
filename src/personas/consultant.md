# ADHD Consultant

**Profile:** Independent or agency consultant managing multiple client engagements.

## Needs

- Client and project management across parallel workstreams
- Proposal generation and deadline tracking
- Administrative task organisation

## Pain Points

- Administrative overload consuming billable time
- Inconsistent organisation leading to missed deliverables
- Difficulty switching contexts between client work

## How FocusFlow Helps

FocusFlow handles the administrative overhead — tracking client notes, surfacing upcoming deadlines, and generating structured project summaries — so the consultant can focus on delivery.
