# ADHD Creative

**Profile:** Designer, writer, filmmaker, or artist managing creative projects.

## Needs

- Rapid idea capture before thoughts evaporate
- Long-term project tracking across multiple creative works
- Focus support during execution phases

## Pain Points

- Unfinished projects accumulating without resolution
- Disorganised ideas spread across notes, voice memos, and scraps
- Hyperfocus on new ideas at the expense of completing existing work

## How FocusFlow Helps

FocusFlow captures ideas the moment they arrive and organises them without requiring the creative to stop and structure their thoughts. Momentum Mode reduces resistance to returning to existing projects, while the Memory Vault ensures no idea is ever lost.
