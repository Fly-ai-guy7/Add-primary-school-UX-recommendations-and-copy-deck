# ADHD Entrepreneur

**Profile:** Business owner managing multiple responsibilities simultaneously.

## Needs

- Task prioritisation across competing demands
- Follow-up tracking for client and team interactions
- Meeting preparation support
- In-session focus support

## Pain Points

- Too many ideas with no clear execution path
- Missed follow-ups damaging relationships and revenue
- Constant context switching destroying deep work

## How FocusFlow Helps

FocusFlow acts as a Chief of Staff — filtering noise, surfacing the highest-priority next action, and ensuring nothing falls through the cracks. The Brain Dump Engine captures ideas without interrupting flow, while Daily Briefings keep the entrepreneur oriented each morning.
