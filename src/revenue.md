# Revenue Model

## Free Tier

Available to all users, no credit card required.

**Includes:**
- Brain Dump Engine
- Focus Mode
- Daily Briefing

---

## Pro Tier — EGP 500/month

For individuals who need the full cognitive support system.

**Includes everything in Free, plus:**
- ADHD Memory Vault
- AI Body Double
- Unlimited focus sessions

---

## Entrepreneur Tier — EGP 1,250/month

For ADHD business owners who need FocusFlow integrated into their business operations.

**Includes everything in Pro, plus:**
- CRM integration
- Calendar integration
- Email assistance
- Business workflow support

---

## Pricing Rationale

The free tier is designed to deliver immediate value and build habit — the two highest barriers for ADHD users adopting new tools. Monetisation occurs once the user has experienced the product's core value and established a routine.
