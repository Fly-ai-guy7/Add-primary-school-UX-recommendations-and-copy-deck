# Time and Revenue Recovery — Interactive Framework

Use this framework to calculate your personal ROI from FocusFlow AI™.

---

## Step 1 — Your Hourly Value

What is your time worth? If you're a consultant, use your billing rate. If you're a business owner, use your estimated revenue per productive hour.

```
My hourly rate: EGP ______

(If unsure: monthly revenue ÷ hours worked = hourly rate)
```

---

## Step 2 — Hours Lost to Executive Dysfunction

How many hours per day do you estimate you lose to:
- Starting tasks (initiation resistance)
- Recovering from interruptions
- Re-organising and re-prioritising (instead of doing)
- Avoidance and procrastination
- Context loss ("where was I?")

```
Hours lost per day: ______

Typical range: 1–4 hours/day for adults with ADHD
Conservative estimate if unsure: 2 hours/day
```

---

## Step 3 — Monthly Cost of Executive Dysfunction

```
Hours lost per day × 20 working days = hours lost per month
Hours lost per month × hourly rate = monthly cost of ADHD friction

Example:
2 hrs/day × 20 days = 40 hrs/month
40 hrs × EGP 500/hr = EGP 20,000/month in lost productivity
```

---

## Step 4 — Conservative Recovery with FocusFlow

FocusFlow is not a cure. A conservative recovery estimate is 30–50% of lost hours.

```
Hours lost per month × 40% recovery = hours recovered

Example:
40 hrs × 40% = 16 hours/month recovered
16 hrs × EGP 500/hr = EGP 8,000/month recovered
```

---

## Step 5 — Net ROI

```
Monthly revenue recovered - FocusFlow Pro cost = Net monthly return

Example:
EGP 8,000 - EGP 500 = EGP 7,500 net monthly return
ROI: EGP 8,000 ÷ EGP 500 = 16x
```
