# Executive Summary

FocusFlow AI™ is an AI-powered executive function operating system designed specifically for individuals with ADHD.

Unlike traditional productivity tools, FocusFlow does not assume users can effectively prioritise, organise, and execute tasks independently. Instead, it acts as an intelligent layer between thought and action — transforming high-speed, non-linear thinking into structured execution.

The platform combines:

- **Conversational AI** — natural language interaction for capturing and processing thoughts
- **Memory systems** — persistent context and retrieval across sessions
- **Accountability mechanisms** — check-ins, body doubling, and progress tracking
- **Task orchestration** — intelligent prioritisation and single-task focus

FocusFlow helps users move from ideas to outcomes.
