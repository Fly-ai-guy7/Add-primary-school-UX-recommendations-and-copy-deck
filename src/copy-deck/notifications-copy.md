# Copy Deck — Notifications

All notification titles are 8 words or fewer. No emojis. No exclamation points.

---

## Scheduled Briefings

| Notification | Title | Body |
|---|---|---|
| Morning briefing (standard) | Your day is ready. | 3 priorities · 2 meetings |
| Morning briefing (light day) | Light day ahead. | 2 priorities to work through |
| Morning briefing (heavy day) | Big day. Here's your focus. | I've surfaced the 3 things that matter most |
| Evening briefing (tasks done) | Good work today. | Wrap up and plan tomorrow |
| Evening briefing (low completion) | Day complete. | Review what happened and plan tomorrow |
| Morning — missed (after 10am) | Still time to plan. | Your briefing is waiting |
