# Email / DM — Cold Outreach to ADHD Professionals

Use these templates when reaching out directly to ADHD creators, coaches, consultants, or community leaders who might become beta users, advocates, or partners.

---

## Template 1 — ADHD Coach / Creator

**Subject:** I built something for the problem you talk about

> Hi [Name],
>
> I follow your content on [platform] — your post about [specific post or topic] was the one that made me think you'd get what I'm building.
>
> I have ADHD. I've built a business with ADHD. And I spent years watching every productivity tool fail me for the same reason — they all require you to be organised before they help you get organised.
>
> I'm building FocusFlow AI™ to fix that. It's an AI executive function system — you brain dump anything on your mind, it prioritises and organises for you, shows you one task at a time, and works alongside you during focus sessions.
>
> I'd love to offer you early Pro access. No pitch, no ask. Just: use it, tell me what's broken, and if you think it's useful, I'd love to know what you think.
>
> Happy to send you more details or jump on a 15-minute call if that's easier.
>
> Bruce Mc.
> Founder, FocusFlow AI™ / Astra Intelligence Services
