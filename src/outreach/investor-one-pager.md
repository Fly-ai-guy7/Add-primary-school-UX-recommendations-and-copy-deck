### The Problem

ADHD affects 5–10 million Egyptians. A significant proportion are entrepreneurs, consultants, and high-functioning professionals who lose 2–4 hours of productive time per day to executive dysfunction — task paralysis, time blindness, context loss, and overwhelm.

Every productivity tool on the market requires users to organise themselves first. For ADHD users, that is an impossible prerequisite.

**The annual productivity cost per ADHD professional:** Estimated EGP 120,000–480,000 in lost output. The EGP 500/month Pro subscription recovers 10–20x its cost conservatively.

---

### The Solution

FocusFlow AI™ removes the organisational prerequisite entirely. Users speak or type anything on their mind. The AI classifies, prioritises, and acts — presenting one task at a time, maintaining all context, and providing active session support.

**8 core modules:** Brain Dump Engine · Focus Mode · Daily Briefing · Memory Vault · AI Body Double · Momentum Mode · Context Recovery · Overwhelm Detection
