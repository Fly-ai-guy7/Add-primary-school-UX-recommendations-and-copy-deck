## Guidelines for Community Posting

- **Always post as yourself (Bruce), not as the company.** Community trust is personal.
- **Never use marketing language** — "revolutionary," "game-changing," "disrupting" — it reads as hollow to ADHD communities who have heard it before.
- **Acknowledge that you have ADHD** — this is the single most important credibility signal.
- **Always offer a direct way to respond** — DM, comment, or link. ADHD users won't search for you.
- **Respond to every comment personally** in the first 48 hours. This is relationship-building, not broadcasting.
