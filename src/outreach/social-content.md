**Post 2 — What Body Doubling Actually Is**

> If you have ADHD, you've probably experienced body doubling without knowing it had a name.
>
> It's the phenomenon where you can't work alone — but the moment someone else is in the room (even on a call, even silent), things start moving.
>
> It works because the social presence activates the ADHD brain's accountability circuits. It's not about external pressure. It's about the nervous system finally having the signal it needs to start.
>
> FocusFlow AI™ has an AI Body Double built in. It opens your session, checks in halfway through, and closes it out with you. It's not a person. But for a lot of ADHD users, it's enough.
>
> Built from personal experience. Beta opening soon.

---

**Post 3 — The Follow-Up Problem**

> The most expensive ADHD symptom for entrepreneurs isn't distraction.
>
> It's the follow-up you forgot.
>
> The client you meant to call back. The proposal you never sent. The email you read and fully intended to answer and somehow never did.
>
> Each one is a revenue event that didn't happen.
>
> FocusFlow AI™ captures every follow-up — in the moment, from a brain dump, from a meeting note — and surfaces it before it's too late.
>
> One of the most common pieces of feedback I've heard from ADHD entrepreneurs: "I knew I needed to do it. I just forgot I knew."
>
> FocusFlow is the system that doesn't forget.
