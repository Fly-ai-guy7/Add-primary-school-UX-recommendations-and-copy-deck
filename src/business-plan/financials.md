### Path to Profitability

At Year 1 end ARR of EGP 4.7M against costs of EGP 1.32M, the business reaches operational profitability within Year 1 on conservative growth assumptions — provided the founder draws a modest salary in Year 1.

## Key Assumptions

1. Free → Pro conversion: 10% of registered users within 6 months of signup
2. Monthly churn: 5% (industry average for early-stage SaaS; target improvement to 3% by Year 2)
3. AI API cost per active user: ~EGP 40/month at scale (declining as models commoditise)
4. No paid acquisition spend in Year 1 — organic and community only
5. Annual plan uptake: 20% of paying users (smooths cash flow)
6. No enterprise or B2B revenue included in projections above
