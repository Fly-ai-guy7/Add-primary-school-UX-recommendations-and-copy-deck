# Executive Summary

## The Company

Astra Intelligence Services is an Egyptian AI company building FocusFlow AI™ — an executive function operating system for people with ADHD.

The company is founder-led, pre-seed, and in active product development as of mid-2026.

---

## The Problem

ADHD affects an estimated 5–10% of the global population. In Egypt alone, that represents between 5 and 10 million people. A significant proportion are professionals — entrepreneurs, consultants, creatives, and freelancers — who manage businesses and client relationships while dealing with executive dysfunction, time blindness, and task paralysis every day.

Existing productivity tools — Notion, Todoist, Trello, Asana — were not designed for ADHD. They require the user to organise themselves before the tool becomes useful. That is precisely the capability ADHD impairs most.

The result: a large, underserved market of high-potential professionals who cycle through tools, never find one that works, and lose significant income and opportunity to the chaos that follows.

---

## The Solution

FocusFlow AI™ removes the organisational prerequisite entirely.

The user speaks or types anything on their mind. FocusFlow classifies, prioritises, and acts — presenting one task at a time, providing AI-assisted focus sessions, maintaining a searchable memory of everything, and detecting overwhelm before it becomes paralysis.

It is not a to-do list. It is an AI Chief of Staff, Project Manager, Accountability Partner, Memory System, and Focus Coach — built for the way ADHD minds actually work.

---

## Market Opportunity

- **Total Addressable Market (TAM):** Global ADHD productivity software market — estimated USD 2.1B by 2028, growing at ~18% CAGR
- **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):** Arabic-speaking and Egyptian professional market — currently underserved with zero ADHD-specific tools in Arabic
- **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM):** Egyptian ADHD professionals (entrepreneurs, consultants, freelancers) — conservative target of 50,000 paying users within 3 years
