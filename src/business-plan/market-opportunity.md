# Market Opportunity

## Global ADHD Market

ADHD affects an estimated 366 million adults worldwide (based on 5% global adult prevalence). The majority are undiagnosed, underdiagnosed, or managing without support.

The global market for ADHD-related software, tools, and services is growing rapidly as:
- Adult ADHD diagnosis rates increase (particularly post-pandemic)
- Remote work removes the external structure that previously masked ADHD challenges
- AI capabilities make personalised, responsive support economically viable for the first time

| Market Segment | Estimated Size |
|---|---|
| Global ADHD productivity software TAM | USD 2.1B by 2028 |
| CAGR | ~18% |
| MENA digital productivity market | USD 340M+ (2025) |
| Arabic-language ADHD tools | **Effectively zero** |

## The Egyptian Opportunity

Egypt is the largest Arabic-speaking country by population and one of the fastest-growing digital economies in Africa and the Middle East.

| Egypt Market Factor | Data |
|---|---|
| Population | ~105 million |
| Internet penetration | ~72% and growing |
| Smartphone penetration | ~68% |
| Estimated ADHD population | 5–10 million |
| Working-age adults with ADHD | ~3–6 million |
| Freelance / self-employed workforce | ~9 million (growing) |
| SaaS adoption among professionals | Rapidly increasing |
| Government AI strategy | Egypt National AI Strategy — active investment |

### Why Egypt Now

- The Egyptian government's National AI Strategy (NAIS) actively promotes AI-native products built in Egypt
- Egyptian Data Residency requirements favour locally built products for enterprise and government sales
- EGP-denominated pricing removes the USD access barrier that excludes most Egyptians from global SaaS tools
- A growing startup ecosystem with active angel investor networks (Cairo Angels, AUC Venture Lab, Flat6Labs)

## Expansion Opportunity

Egypt is the beachhead. The Arabic-speaking ADHD market extends across:

- Saudi Arabia (35M population, high smartphone penetration, large expat and entrepreneur community)
- UAE (growing startup and freelance ecosystem)
- Morocco, Jordan, Kuwait — established digital middle classes

A single Arabic-language ADHD platform has a natural path to 400M+ Arabic speakers — a market with zero dedicated competition today.
