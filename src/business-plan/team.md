# Team

## Founder and CEO — Bruce Mc.

Bruce Mc. is the founder and CEO of Astra Intelligence Services and the creator of FocusFlow AI™.

He brings a rare combination of lived experience, entrepreneurial background, and technical vision to the problem. FocusFlow AI™ was not built from market research alone — it was built from the personal experience of an entrepreneur with ADHD who cycled through every productivity tool available and found that none of them worked.

**Why this founder for this product:**

- **Lived experience** — Bruce has ADHD. He is not building for a market he researched; he is building for himself and people who share his experience. This is the most credible possible founder-market fit.
- **Entrepreneurial background** — Building a company while managing ADHD executive function challenges gives Bruce a direct understanding of the pain points FocusFlow solves for its core user persona.
- **Product vision** — The FocusFlow PRD, UX design, copy deck, and system architecture reflect a detailed, coherent product philosophy that goes well beyond surface-level feature lists.
- **Egypt-based** — Building from within the target market, with local networks, cultural understanding, and alignment with the Egyptian National AI Strategy.
