# Go-to-Market Strategy

## Phase 1 — Community Ignition (Months 1–6)

### Objective
Build a core community of ADHD professionals in Egypt and the Arabic-speaking world who become users, advocates, and the foundation for word-of-mouth growth.

### Channels

**ADHD Community Groups**
- Egyptian and Arabic ADHD Facebook groups (several have 10,000–50,000+ members)
- Reddit communities: r/ADHD, r/ADHDers, r/Entrepreneur
- LinkedIn ADHD professional communities

**Founder-Led Content**
Bruce Mc. publishes content from personal experience — the founder with ADHD building a tool for ADHD. This is a credible, differentiated voice in a space where most content is from neurotypical researchers or clinicians. Platforms: LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok.

**ADHD Influencer and Advocate Partnerships**
Identify 5–10 Arabic-language ADHD creators and coaches. Offer early Pro access in exchange for honest reviews and community introductions. No paid sponsorships at this stage — earned trust only.

**Beta Waitlist Launch**
A simple landing page with the beta waitlist. Goal: 1,000 waitlist sign-ups before public launch. Use waitlist as a feedback and co-creation community.

### KPIs — Phase 1
- 1,000 waitlist sign-ups
- 500 beta users activated
- 30-day retention > 40%
- NPS > 50

---

## Phase 2 — Retention and Referral (Months 7–18)

### Objective
Convert community members to paying Pro users. Build the referral loop.

### Channels

**Referral Programme**
Pro users get 1 month free for each successful referral. Referred users convert at 3–5x the rate of cold traffic. This is the most capital-efficient growth lever at this stage.

**Arabic SEO Content**
Long-form Arabic content targeting high-intent searches:
- "كيف أتعامل مع ADHD في العمل" (How to manage ADHD at work)
- "تطبيقات للإنتاجية مع ADHD" (Productivity apps for ADHD)
- "علاج التشتت والتأخير" (Treating distraction and procrastination)

This traffic compounds over time and converts at high rates because users arrive already aware of their problem.

**Email Sequences**
Onboarding sequences, feature discovery emails, and re-engagement campaigns for churned users. All written in the FocusFlow voice — warm, direct, non-judgemental.

### KPIs — Phase 2
- 2,500 paying users
- Monthly churn < 5%
- Referral rate > 15% of new sign-ups
- MRR: EGP 1.25M

---

## Phase 3 — Scale and Expansion (Year 2+)

### Objective
Expand beyond Egypt into the Arabic-speaking market. Add the B2B channel.

### Channels

**Gulf Market Entry (UAE, Saudi Arabia)**
Localise pricing to AED and SAR. Partner with Gulf-based ADHD coaches and productivity consultants. Existing Arabic-language content travels.

**B2B / Corporate Channel**
Target HR directors and employee wellness leads at Egyptian and Gulf corporations. Position FocusFlow as an employee wellness benefit that measurably reduces presenteeism for ADHD employees.

**Accelerator and Partner Networks**
Apply to Egyptian and regional accelerators (Flat6Labs, AUC Venture Lab, WAMDA) for visibility, mentorship, and network access. Not primarily for capital at this stage.

### KPIs — Phase 3
- 8,000 paying users
- Gulf market: 15% of user base
- First B2B contract signed
- MRR: EGP 5M+
