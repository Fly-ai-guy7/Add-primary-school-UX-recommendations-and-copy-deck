# Business Model

## Revenue Model — Freemium SaaS

FocusFlow AI™ operates on a freemium subscription model. The free tier is a genuine product experience — not a crippled demo — designed to build habit and trust before monetisation.

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Target User |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | EGP 0 | EGP 0 | First-time users, habit formation |
| **Pro** | EGP 500/month | EGP 5,000/year | Individual ADHD professionals |
| **Entrepreneur** | EGP 1,250/month | EGP 12,500/year | ADHD business owners |

### What Each Tier Includes

**Free**
- Brain Dump Engine (unlimited)
- Focus Mode
- Daily Briefing (Morning + Evening)

**Pro — EGP 500/month**
Everything in Free, plus:
- ADHD Memory Vault
- AI Body Double
- Unlimited focus sessions
- Momentum Mode
- Overwhelm Detection
- Context Recovery

**Entrepreneur — EGP 1,250/month**
Everything in Pro, plus:
- CRM integration
- Calendar sync
- Email assistance
- Business workflow support

## Unit Economics

### Key Metrics (Targets)

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Free → Pro conversion rate | 10% |
| Monthly churn (Pro) | < 5% |
| Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) — blended | EGP 550/month |
| Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) | EGP 300–500 |
| Lifetime Value (LTV) — Pro at 24 months avg. | EGP 12,000 |
| LTV:CAC ratio | ~25:1 |

### Gross Margin

SaaS gross margins for AI-enabled products typically run 60–75% after AI API costs, hosting, and infrastructure. FocusFlow targets 65%+ gross margin at scale.

## Growth Model

**Phase 1 — Community-Led (Year 1)**

Growth through ADHD community channels: Facebook groups, Reddit communities, TikTok/Instagram content, ADHD influencer partnerships. Zero paid acquisition. Trust is built by being genuinely useful to a community that has been underserved by every other tool.

**Phase 2 — Referral + Content (Year 2)**

A structured referral programme and SEO-driven content strategy targeting high-intent ADHD searches in Arabic. Each active Pro user referred converts at a materially higher rate than cold traffic.

**Phase 3 — B2B / Enterprise Channel (Year 3+)**

Corporate HR and employee wellness programmes. Companies with ADHD-identified employees (a growing category post-pandemic) can provide FocusFlow as a workplace benefit. Higher contract value, lower churn, longer sales cycle.
